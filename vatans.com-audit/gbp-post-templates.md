## Notes
- **Character limit:** GBP posts allow up to 1,500 characters. All templates above are well under that.
- **CTA button:** When posting, GBP offers optional "Call Now," "Book," "Order online," and "Learn more" buttons. Use **"Order online"** (vatans.com/order.html?loc=ew) for food posts and **"Book"** (vatans.com/reservations.html) for event/catering posts.
- **Don't post the same template two weeks in a row.** Rotate A → B → C → D → E → F → G → back to A, etc.
- **GBP posts expire after 6 months.** Google may show an "update expired" notice — just post a new one to replace it.
