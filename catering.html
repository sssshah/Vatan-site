FAQ

Common Questions

How far in advance should I place my catering order?+
We recommend placing your order at least 48 hours in advance to ensure availability and proper preparation. Larger events may require additional notice — please contact us early for weddings and corporate events.
Do you offer delivery?+
Yes! We provide home and venue delivery within a 5-mile radius of each location. If you need delivery beyond this range, contact us to discuss special arrangements and pricing.
Can I customize the catering menu?+
Absolutely. We offer fully customized menu options to suit your event's needs and preferences. Whether you have specific dietary requirements or a particular cuisine in mind, we'll tailor the menu accordingly.
Do you cater for Jain and Swaminarayan diets?+
Yes — this is one of our specialties. We have extensive experience preparing food for Jain and Swaminarayan dietary requirements. Please inform us when placing your order so we can plan accordingly.
What is the minimum order for catering?+
Minimum order quantities vary by package. Contact us with your guest count and event details and we'll work out the best arrangement for you — we cater to events both large and small.
Do you provide serving equipment and setup?+
For larger events, we can discuss setup arrangements. Please mention this when requesting your quote so we can include it in the planning and pricing.
