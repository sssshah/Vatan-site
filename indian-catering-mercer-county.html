Dietary Options

Jain & Swaminarayan Catering Available

Jain Menu

No Root Vegetables

Entire menus prepared without onion, garlic, potatoes, carrots, beets, or any root vegetables. Standard Jain preparation — not an accommodation, but a dedicated menu track.

Swaminarayan Menu

No Onion or Garlic

Swaminarayan-compliant menus prepared without onion or garlic throughout. Both individual items and full thali-style service available.

All catering from Vatan is 100% vegetarian — no meat, fish, or eggs. For events where multiple dietary standards must be accommodated simultaneously (e.g., a mixed Jain and non-Jain guest list), we can prepare separate preparation tracks within the same catering order.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Vatan provide Indian catering near Princeton NJ?

Yes. Our East Windsor kitchen at 761 Route 33 West caters throughout Mercer County — Princeton, West Windsor, Plainsboro, Hightstown, Hamilton, Robbinsville, Cranbury, Monroe Township, and South Brunswick. Contact info@vatans.com or call (609) 336-7333 to discuss your event.

Is Jain catering available in Mercer County NJ?

Yes. Fully Jain-compliant catering — no root vegetables, no onion, garlic, potatoes, carrots, or beets — is a standard offering. Swaminarayan catering (no onion or garlic) is also available. These are not special requests; they are dedicated preparation tracks we run alongside the standard menu.

What types of events does Vatan cater in Mercer County?

Weddings, engagement ceremonies, navratri and Diwali celebrations, corporate lunches, birthday and anniversary parties, university events, and private family gatherings. Both buffet-style and individually packaged service available.

How far in advance should I book catering near Princeton NJ?

For weddings and events over 50 guests, 4–6 weeks in advance is recommended. For corporate lunches and smaller gatherings under 30 guests, 1–2 weeks is typically sufficient. Contact us as early as possible for weekend dates, which book fastest.
