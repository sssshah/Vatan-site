Vatan Indian Vegetarian Cuisine & Bakery has been catering authentic Gujarati, Kathiyawadi, and South Indian cuisine to Hudson County events since 2009. Based on 808 Newark Ave, Jersey City, NJ 07306 — in the heart of Jersey City's Indian food corridor — our catering team reaches Hoboken, Newark, Bayonne, Union City, Weehawken, and communities throughout Hudson County. Catering FAQ

How do I get a catering quote?

Call us at (201) 839-5426 or email info@vatans.com with your event date, guest count, location, and any dietary requirements. You can also visit our full catering page for package details.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend contacting us at least 1–2 weeks ahead for smaller events and 3–4 weeks for weddings or large gatherings. For peak season events (summer weddings, Diwali, Navratri), earlier is better.

Do you offer Jain or Swaminarayan catering?

Yes. We have extensive experience with both Jain (no root vegetables) and Swaminarayan (no onion or garlic) menus. Please let us know your requirements when inquiring. Learn more about our Jain & Swaminarayan options in Jersey City →

Do you cater for Indian weddings in the Jersey City area?

Yes. Vatan has catered for weddings, engagement parties, and large cultural gatherings across Hudson County. Our menus are fully customizable — from live chaat and dosa stations to full thali service. Call (201) 839-5426 to discuss your event.
