Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about dining at Vatan

Dietary & Menu

Is Vatan 100% vegetarian?

Yes. Vatan is a pure vegetarian restaurant — no meat, no chicken, no fish, no eggs, ever. Our kitchen has never prepared non-vegetarian food.

Does Vatan have Jain-friendly food?

Yes. We offer a dedicated Jain Thali ($20.99) and many Jain-friendly items throughout the menu, prepared without root vegetables (no onion, garlic, potatoes, carrots, or beets). Jain items are marked (J) on our menu. See our Jain & Swaminarayan page →

Does Vatan have a Swaminarayan Thali?

Yes. Our Swaminarayan Thali ($21.99) is prepared per Swaminarayan dietary guidelines — no onion or garlic. It includes 2 vegetables, kathol, rice, khichdi, kadhi, dal, pickle, jaggery, sweet, raita, papad, and farsan.

Are vegan options available?

Yes. Many dishes are naturally vegan — including most chaat items, lentil preparations, vegetable curries, rice dishes, and street food. Paneer, ghee, and yogurt-based dishes are the main non-vegan items. Our staff can help identify vegan-friendly choices.

What is a Thali and what does it include?

A Thali is a traditional Indian meal served on a large round platter with multiple small dishes — curries, dal, vegetables, rice, bread, pickles, chutneys, and a sweet. At Vatan, our Vatan Special Thali ($20.99) includes 3 vegetables, kathol, rice, khichdi, kadhi, dal, pickle, jaggery, garlic chutney, sweet, raita, papad, and farsan — with your choice of rotla, roti, poori, methi thepla, or puran poli. See our full Thali page for all varieties.

What is Kathiyawadi cuisine?

Kathiyawadi cuisine comes from the Kathiawar peninsula of Gujarat, India. It's bold, rustic, and typically spicier than standard Gujarati food — featuring dishes like Misal Pav, Sev Usal, Dabeli, and dry vegetable preparations with pungent spices. Vatan is one of the few New Jersey restaurants that specializes in this regional style.

What is the price range at Vatan?

Vatan is a mid-range restaurant ($$). Chaat and street food items start around $7–$10. Thalis range from $16.99 (Chaat Thali) to $21.99 (Swaminarayan Thali). Main course dishes are generally $12–$18.

Ordering & Reservations

Can I order Vatan food online?

Yes. Place a direct pickup order at vatans.com/order.html — no delivery fees, restaurant calls to confirm within 15 minutes. Vatan is also available on Uber Eats, Seamless, and GrubHub for delivery.

How long does a pickup order take?

After placing your order online, the restaurant will call you within 15 minutes to confirm and provide an estimated pickup time. We recommend ordering at least 30 minutes before your desired pickup time.

Do I need a reservation?

Reservations are recommended, especially on weekends and for groups of 4 or more. Walk-ins are welcome based on availability. Reserve online or call your preferred location.

Does Vatan offer catering?

Yes. We provide full-service Indian vegetarian catering for weddings, corporate events, religious functions, and private parties across New Jersey and surrounding areas. Visit our catering page to request a quote.
