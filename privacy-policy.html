Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Vatan Indian Vegetarian Cuisine & Bakery ("we," "us," "our") uses Google Business Profile data in connection with our internal review management tool.

What data we access

Through the Google Business Profile API, we access:

  • Customer reviews left on our Google Business Profile (reviewer name, star rating, review text, review date)
  • The ability to post replies to those reviews on our Google Business Profile

How we use this data

We use this data solely to:

  • Monitor and respond to customer reviews on our own Google Business Profile
  • Generate and, where appropriate, automatically post review replies
  • Maintain an internal log of reviews and our responses for our own records

How we store this data

Review data (reviewer name, rating, review text, our reply, and status) is stored in a private, internally-managed spreadsheet accessible only to authorized staff of our business. We do not sell, rent, or share this data with third parties, and we do not use it for advertising or marketing purposes unrelated to responding to the reviews themselves.

Data retention

We retain review data for as long as necessary to maintain accurate records of our customer interactions. You may request removal of specific data by contacting us using the information below.

Third-party services

This tool uses the following third-party services to function:

  • Google Business Profile API — to retrieve reviews and post replies
  • n8n — workflow automation platform used to run this process
  • Anthropic's Claude API — used to help draft review replies

These providers process data only as needed to perform the functions above, under their own respective privacy and security practices.

Your rights

If you are a customer who left a review and would like to know what data we have associated with your review, or would like it removed from our internal records, please contact us at the email below.

Contact us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at:

info@vatans.com

This policy applies only to our use of Google Business Profile data for review management purposes and does not cover other aspects of our website or business operations.
