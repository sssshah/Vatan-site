Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a pure vegetarian restaurant near BAPS Akshardham in Robbinsville, NJ?

Yes — Vatan Indian Vegetarian Restaurant in East Windsor is just 10–15 minutes from BAPS Swaminarayan Akshardham via Route 33 West. It is 100% pure vegetarian with no meat on the premises.

Is Vatan Jain and Swaminarayan friendly?

Yes. Vatan is a 100% vegetarian restaurant. No meat, no eggs, and no cross-contamination. The menu includes options suitable for Swaminarayan and Jain dietary requirements. Our team can guide you through the menu.

Can Vatan accommodate large groups from the temple?

Yes. We welcome large groups and families. For groups of 10 or more, please call ahead at (609) 336-7333 so we can arrange appropriate seating. Catering is also available for temple and community events.

What are the hours of Vatan East Windsor?

Tuesday–Sunday: Lunch 11:00 AM – 3:00 PM, Dinner 4:30 PM – 9:30 PM. Closed Mondays. We recommend reserving a table on weekends when dining with a large group.

Does Vatan offer catering for temple community events?

Yes. We cater satsangs, community dinners, religious celebrations, weddings, and cultural events throughout central NJ. Contact us at (609) 336-7333 or info@vatans.com to discuss your event.
